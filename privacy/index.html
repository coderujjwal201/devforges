Privacy Statement

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

1. Zero Remote Storage

Our core policy is simple: your data belongs to you. All values, formats, files, regular expressions, and parameters you input compile locally inside your browser memory context. We do not store or transmit inputs to external backend endpoints.

2. Local Storage Caching

To provide a premium developer experience, DEVFORGES caches parameters (such as theme configurations, active workspace favorites, and input fields) inside your browser's local sandbox storage (localStorage).

This data is kept locally on your machine, and you can clear it at any time by wiping your browser site settings or clicking the **Clear** controls inside specific tools.

3. Third-Party Analytics & Cookies

DEVFORGES does not deploy tracking pixels, cookies, or tracking APIs. We utilize static assets to prevent client footprint tracking, ensuring absolute privacy.

4. Security Architecture Audits

DEVFORGES operates completely offline. You can audit network isolation by monitoring devtools logs or severing your internet connection while formatting.
