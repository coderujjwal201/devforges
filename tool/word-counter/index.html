Word Counter & Keyword Density Analyzer

Evaluate text layouts for standard content metrics. Word Counter tracks character counts, estimated reading time, speaking speed coefficients, sentence divisions, and lists density metrics detailing high-frequency words.

How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text corpus inside the left textarea editor.
  2. Review live calculations displayed on the right: total word counts, space-adjusted character volumes, estimated lines, and sentences.
  3. Check the **Top Word Density** grid list. Highly recurring keywords (excluding generic English structures under 3 characters) display their occurrence frequency, helping refine SEO optimization copy.
  4. Click **Copy Statistics** to copy a structured text report summary.
